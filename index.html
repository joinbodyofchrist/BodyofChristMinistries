Our Statement of Faith

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God, serving as the final authority for faith, doctrine, and Christian living.

God

We believe in one God, eternally existing as Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

We believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God, fully divine, who lived a sinless life and willingly gave Himself on the cross as a sacrifice for sin. Through His death and resurrection, salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, resulting in regeneration, justification, and adoption into God’s family. Jesus Christ is our Lord and Savior, and we are committed to following Him in obedience and humility.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the present and active work of the Holy Spirit, who convicts, regenerates, equips, and empowers believers for godly living and service. We seek to be led by the Holy Spirit in all areas of life and ministry.

Baptism

We believe in water baptism by immersion as a public testimony of faith in Christ and obedience to His command.

Spiritual Gifts

We believe in the ongoing operation of the gifts of the Holy Spirit for the edification of the church and the advancement of God’s kingdom, including:

  • Healing
  • Prophecy
  • Tongues
  • Interpretation of tongues

Ministry and Service

We understand that leading a ministry is both a privilege and a responsibility. We are committed to living lives that reflect Christlike character, sound doctrine, and moral integrity. We desire to serve with teachable hearts, under spiritual authority, and in unity with the body of Christ.

Our Commitment

It is our sincere desire to use the gifts God has given us to serve His church, build up others, and glorify Jesus Christ in all that we do. We are committed to continued spiritual growth through prayer, Scripture, fellowship, and accountability.

Our Prayer

We pray that our lives and ministry will honor the Lord and advance His kingdom.
